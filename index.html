The East Coast region (ECPM) spans four states, collectively forming a critical ecological corridor along the South China Sea.
State Biodiversity Contributions
Kelantan: Mangrove-fringed coasts & productive fisheries.
Terengganu: Coral-rich islands & major turtle nesting areas.
Pahang: High-cover reefs & extensive mangrove habitats.
Johor: Archipelagos with notable seagrass & coral diversity.
